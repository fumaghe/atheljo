STORViX Launches Avalon WebApp: Data Control, Redefined

From Insight to Action — The Future of Real-Time Telemetry Begins Now

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE: Lund, Sweden – April 2, 2025

STORViX is proud to unveil Avalon, a next-generation web application engineered to transform the way organizations interact with real-time system data. Designed for internal operations and analytics, Avalon is the culmination of strategic restructuring from the original Archimedes Analyzer, offering an intuitive, secure, and dynamic platform for enterprise telemetry management.

What’s Available in the First Demo

  • Secure OTP Login & Password Change: A robust multi-step authentication system, including dynamic password strength evaluation.
  • Dashboard: Immediate access to real-time system metrics, interactive charts, and alerts overview.
  • Analytics Section: Explore detailed breakdowns by company, system, and pool — complete with State Vector Analysis and a fully functional Health Score engine.
  • Role-Based Account Management: A pyramid structure lets administrators configure sub-accounts, assign company visibility, and manage granular permissions.
  • Settings & Support: Password update, feature toggles, and access to in-app documentation for an optimal user experience.

Why Avalon?

Avalon is more than a dashboard — it’s a strategic hub for operational intelligence. It bridges data silos by integrating SQL and NoSQL databases into one seamless interface, offering both depth and clarity in every interaction.

System Health at a Glance

The Health Score system analyzes key metrics — capacity, performance, telemetry, snapshots, and MUP — to deliver a real-time indicator of system efficiency. This feature helps teams prioritize actions and maintain optimal performance.

Security You Can Trust

Built with enterprise-grade security protocols, Avalon ensures all user access is authenticated, logged, and protected. Password policies and secure login flows uphold the platform’s integrity and user trust.

Flexible Subscription Plans

Whether you need the essentials or advanced analytics, Avalon offers three subscription tiers — Essential, Advantage, and Premiere — each crafted to unlock relevant features for every type of user.

Where We're Headed

While this release focuses on core functionality, our roadmap includes Forecasting, Advanced Reports, Enhanced Alert History, and full customization across analytics views. With every update, Avalon moves closer to becoming the all-in-one telemetry solution for enterprises.

Our official roadmap will be published soon with details on upcoming features.

Future Integrations

Avalon will soon support predictive analytics and machine learning modules, leveraging its modular architecture for scalable deployments across multiple environments.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where can I access the app? Avalon runs on any modern browser and is available at avalon.storvix.eu.
  • What can you do with Avalon? Manage and monitor telemetry data in real time, generate reports, and analyze critical metrics.
  • Some features are blurred. Why? This may be due to subscription limitations or because certain features are still under development.
  • How does the subscription work? Avalon offers three tiers: Essential, Advantage, and Premiere — each unlocking different feature sets.
  • Does it support real-time data? Yes, real-time data access is fully supported.
  • What features are available at launch? Dashboards, telemetry analysis, report generation, and alert management.
  • What will be released next? Enhanced reporting and advanced analytics tools are the next priorities.
  • How often are updates released? Regular updates are rolled out based on feedback and development priorities.
  • Where can I send feedback? Reach us via email at info@storvix.eu.
  • What happens to Merlin? MerlinDB remains the core storage for telemetry data.
  • What about Archimedes? Archimedes has evolved and its core features are being re-integrated into Avalon.
  • How do Merlin and Archimedes work with Avalon? Merlin stores raw data; Archimedes processes it and feeds Avalon via Firestore.
  • Why Firestore? Firestore enables fast, scalable access to telemetry data and supports long-term analysis. A future migration to AlloyDB is planned for enhanced performance.
  • Where’s the documentation? Access the documentation through the Support section in the app. A user manual is also in development.
  • Why the name Avalon? It continues the “Sword in the Stone” theme, referencing the legendary island of Arthurian lore.
